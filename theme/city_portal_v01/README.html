CITY PORTAL THEME (VERSION 1.1)

This theme is based on Moodle Standard theme and styled to match new City portal theme. It was created by Mike Hughes January 2010.

All styles are in one file: styles_master.css with additional styles in print.css

By default, standard styles are automatically loaded before any other themes, so that all custom themes need to do is to define their own styles to augment or overwrite particular standard styles.

In this way custom themes are not "left behind" by Moodle upgrades.
