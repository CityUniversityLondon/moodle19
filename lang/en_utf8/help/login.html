Logging in

You should login using your standard City University username and password.

Make sure you are using the correct username and password, and that you have cookies enabled in your browser.

If you cannot login or you think you may have forgotten your password then please contact the IT Service Desk:

  • Contact hours: Monday to Friday 8:00 – 20:30 (Term Time), 8:00 – 20:30 (Out of Term)
  • In person: IT Service Desk (Northampton Square, Room E101)
  • Online: www.city.ac.uk/itservicedesk
  • Phone: Ext 8181 or 020 7040 8181

While IT Services provides support for most students, certain departments and schools have their own IT systems and provide specialist support for them. Please see the Local Support pages for information on how to contact your local support team.
